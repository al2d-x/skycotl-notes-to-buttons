Sky: Notes → Buttons

Converts saved HTML sheets from “Sky Music Sheets” (https://sky-music.github.io/) into
controller or keyboard button charts for Sky: Children of the Light.

This fan-made tool is not affiliated with thatgamecompany or the developers of Sky.

Usage:
 - Choose a saved HTML music sheet from Sky Music.
 - Pick a matching button profile (Xbox, PlayStation, Switch, or Keyboard layout).
 - Click Start to generate a button chart version.

You can view attribution details for icon sets under Help → Licenses.
